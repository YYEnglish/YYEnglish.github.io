Description
The TX-P42ST30 uses innovative 3D NeoPDP technology to bring you an incredibly immersive experience when watching 3D content. The fast switching phosphors used in the Neo Plasma panels and an original light-emitting process minimise crosstalk, delivering clear 3D images and ensures perfect viewing. Its state-of-the-art technology will deliver you amazing picture quality in 2D as well. The 600Hz Intelligent Frame Creation provides fantastic resolution even during fast moving scenes – something football fans and film buffs will both appreciate. This TV features an integrated Freeview HD tuner which will allow you to watch subscription-free HD broadcasts. To watch Freeview HD broadcasts you’ll need to check that you can receive it in your area. Click here to find out more about Freeview HD. Sound is enhanced with its in-built V-Audio Surround sound technology, while the 24p Real Cinema settings offers judder-free playback, giving a smooth ‘film’ look. The 4 HDMI sockets allow you to hook-up your Blu-ray player, satellite receiver and games consoles with ease. VIERA Connect will allow you to enjoy videos, music, sports, games, and a variety of other content on demand via your large-screen TV. Simply connect to the internet via a wireless dongle (available separately) and you’re away. And let’s not forget that the TX-P42ST30 is also DNLA (Digital Living Network Alliance) compatible. This enables you to access your photos and movies from a compatible device such as your PC across your home network. For instance, you could play your friends the latest DivX movie you’ve just downloaded, even though your computer is upstairs in the spare bedroom. You can also easily record your favourite TV programmes, films and other content directly onto an external USB hard disk drive. Recordings are easily controlled using the remote control and you’ll even be able to rewind live TV programmes, reliving great moments or for catching up if your phone rings during the show.
